Dogs

Introduction

Dogs are my favorite animal, because I have always loved how adorable and loyal they are. I have a dog myself, and she's a cheagle (chihuahua-beagle mix). She is just over a year old, and ever since we got her, I felt like my overall happiness/mood increased to the plus side. I've never been happier everyday once I got home to see her waiting for me patiently with her tail wagging.

Diversity and Activity

Every dog person has a different preference. One thing I love about dogs are their variety. Take a chihuahua and a great dane, for instance. Each dog has its own personality, which makes them even more loveable. They also come in all sorts of colors, to tend to each persons' desires. Everyone has their own favorite kind of dog, which is why they are very diverse. There are countless options when choosing a dog to add to your family, and each one shows you a ton of affection.

Dogs are known for being playful. Each dog comes with a bundle of fun because of the amount of energy they have. They always enjoy any game they partake in, and this energy keeps them active, which ultimately results in more happy/joyful memories. This also pushes you to exercise more because of them since you have to walk them a few times everyday. Studies show that people's health overall increased because of the amount of exercise done from walking/playing with your dog.

Loyalty

Dogs are known as "Man's best friend" for a reason. They love their owner since day 1, and don't leave their side. They provide you with company at all times, preventing you from any loneliness. Researchers explored how dogs would react to their owners when they were upset, and compared that to how they would react with complete strangers when they were upset. In the end, whether the owner or stranger cried, the dog would nuzzle and sniff each alike. Since dogs love their owner, they endure through whatever to stay at your side, which is truly an amazing thing.
