Neurology: Study of the Brain

Neurology falls in the study of medicine, as it is involved in the area of neuroscience, and focuses on the brain, spinal cord, and the peripheral nerves.

I am interested in studying neurology because I would like to study medicine when I get older, and I'm interested in studying the brain. I think it is an amazing opportunity to be able to learn how the brain functions, and even operating on one. Most importantly, I would like to save lives, and help others with any neurological diseases or disorders.

Detailed Information

A neurologist is responsible for diagnosing their patients with any medical issues they may have according to their medical history, as well as being able to schedule appointments for patients in need of surgery, and interpreting neurological test results.

The average salary of a neurologist is $291,300. Neurologists may earn between $221,187-$387,969.
