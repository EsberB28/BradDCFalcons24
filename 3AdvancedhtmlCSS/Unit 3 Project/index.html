Population

What is population?

Population is the entirety of inhabitants occupying a certain area (different countries or just the world in general) which is always changed and modified. This is done based on increasing (births and immigration) or decreasing (deaths or emigration) modifications.


So... what is this use of knowledge useful for? Why is it important to know your population density?

Well... knowing and understanding the population densities of different areas are very important, because they hold the given information that tells the government what necessities are needed to be met for everyone in that area to stay alive. This means that the government provides different areas with different ammounts of food based on how many people live in that area.

Another reason the government uses population density data is to identify the overall ammount of people from each culture who live in an area. They also determine more specific details, such as their age, sex, occupation, language, and so on. Population density data is a fancy way to say the counting of people in different areas.
