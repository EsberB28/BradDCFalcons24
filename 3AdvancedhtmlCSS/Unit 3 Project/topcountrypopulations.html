Population of Top 10 Countries

Population is always changing. This is based on people's interests of whether they stay to live in a certain country or not. As of 2025, the top 10 countries with the highest populations are:

  1. India (1,463,865,525)
  2. China (1,416,096,094)
  3. United States (347,275,807)
  4. Indonesia (285,721,236)
  5. Pakistan (255,219,554)
  6. Nigeria (237,527,782)
  7. Brazil (212,812,405)
  8. Bangladesh (175,686,899)
  9. Russia (143,997,393)
  10. Ethiopia (135,472,051)
These are the biggest countries in the world, which makes sense why they have the biggest populations.

Some reasons these countries could have such a large population include multiple different factors. To start off, birth rates could be increased in other areas, which leads to a higher popuation density. Some other reasons besides the main one listed above include:

  • Tourist Attractions
  • Economic factors (urban vs suburban)
  • Historical factors
