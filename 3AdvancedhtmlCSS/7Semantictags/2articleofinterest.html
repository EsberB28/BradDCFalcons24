What is Art?

Art is a way to depict creativity on a board, where one can color their board as they please to form a picture of some sort. There are many different kinds of art, and each one appeals to us in a different way.

The Different Kinds of Art

There are multiple different kinds of art, some including visual art, in which the art you observe is visually pleasing, and abstract art, where there is no real form in their picture. Art can range from multiple differnt things, including working with clay, and even styling things online.

Famous Artists

There were many famous artists who created pieces with us today. One of them was Leonardo da Vinci, and he created the Mona Lisa. Vincent Van Gogh created Starry Night, and Pablo Picasso created Guernica. These artists as well as many more contributed to the art world by prodiving examples of what art is, and how creative you have to be to do it.
