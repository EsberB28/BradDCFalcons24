Artificial Intelligence

May 29, 2025 by Brad Esber

Artificial Intelligence, or AI, is one of the newest introductions of the 21st century. AI was developed to help us with multiple different complicated procedures. Some of these include assisting us in the medical field, providing us with clear and accurate information within seconds, and even being constructed to test safety features. AI is primarily known for:

  • Quick answers
  • Procedures; it helps those who work in the medical fied, but also those who test for safety in automobiles, and so much more
  • Its many upgrades that would constantly make it even better than before, and help us with even more in the future.

AI is currently being developed by the whole world, and many different countries have completely different designs; some of this AI is found globally, such as online through Chat GPT, and physical models, such as test dummies. This, in the future, will be a major plus for us, as it would make our lives safer and easier.
