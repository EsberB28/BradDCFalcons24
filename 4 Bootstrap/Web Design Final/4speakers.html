Fifa Presidents

Fifa has been around for over 100 years. Below are all of the official FIFA presidents.

Robert Guérin

Robert Guérin (1876–1952) is the official founder of FIFA, and became its first president. He was french, and was responsible for promoting it, and keeping the organization strong. He was in office for over 2 years.

Daniel Burley Woolfall

Daniel Burley Woolfall (1852–1918) was the 2nd president of FIFA. He was english, and was responsible for establishing the rules in soccer (Laws of the Game). He was in office for over 12 years.

Jules Rimet

Jules Rimet (1873–1956) was the 3rd president of FIFA. He was french, and was responsible for hosting the first World Cup in July 13, 1930. He was in office for over 33 years (longest-serving president).

Rodolphe Seeldrayers

Rodolphe Seeldrayers (1876–1955) was the 4th president of FIFA. He was belgian, and was responsible for handling the conflict of "false" amateurs entering fifa. He was in office for a year.

Arthur Drewry

Arthur Drewry (1891–1961) was the 5th president of FIFA. He was english, and was responsible for bringing back the British associations into FIFA. He was in office for over 4 years.

Sir Stanley Rous

Sir Stanley Rous (1895–1986) was the 6th president of FIFA. He was english, and was responsible for rewriting the Laws of the game, and making the World Cup a huge spectacle. He was in office for over 12 years.

João Havelange

João Havelange (1916–2016) was the 7th president of FIFA. He was brazilian, and was responsible for expanding the World Cup from 16 teams to 32. He was in office for over 24 years.

Joseph S. Blatter

Joseph S. Blatter (1936-present) was the 8th president of FIFA. He's swiss, and was responsible for strengthening soccer development, creating additional programs. He was in office for over 17 years. Contact!

Gianni Infantino

Gianni Infantino (1970-present) is the present-day president of FIFA. He's swiss and italian, and was responsible for making the World Cup something for everyone, and planning venues. He's still in office. Contact!
