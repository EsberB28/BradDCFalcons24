Schedule

June 11, 2026 – July 19, 2026

Fifa World Cup (as mentioned before) happens every 4 years. It all takes place for about one month, and each year the countries competing face different teams; this ensures fair games every year.

Robert Guerin was the original founder of the fifa company, but over the years, new presidents took over. Today, Gianni Infantino is the present president of fifa. He has been in office since February 26, 2016, and has been re-elected for additional terms.

Venue

June 11, 2026 – July 19, 2026

The Fifa 2026 World Cup will take place in three different locations:

  • United States
  • Canada
  • Mexico

The following lists explains how many games each country hosts:

  • U.S. - 78 Games
  • Mexico - 13 Games
  • Canada - 13 Games

In total, there will be 104 matches. This is the first World Cup where three countries host a World Cup jointly. Most of these games are just to decide who will qualify into the quarter finals, where the real fun begins.
