Backstreet Boys

Backstreet Boys is my favorite band, because I've heard many of their songs before, the most famous one being "I Want it That Way".

I first heard this as a kid, and, on my first time hearing it, I was attached to the song. The beat never gets old, and the pitches of their voices during the song is perfection.

I personally don't think that the Backstreet boys will ever get old, because their iconic music will continue to be passed down through each generation to come. I think their music is one of the highest regarded songs in the world.
